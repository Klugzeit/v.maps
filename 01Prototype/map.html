Welcome to TheRiver! The first step is to choose the categories that you are interested in for your trip: Create, Stay, Join, Meet Just click on the markers over each community!
Next, you can choose in which months you want to take your backpack and see the world.
Use our slider located at the top right corner of the site!
Click on the markers to select where you want to go. A new window will open with information about the community. If you like it, you can add it to your backpack.
Remember to choose only three communities for your pilgrimage!
Open your backpack to see the possible dates in which you can visit the communities.
You can also delete your choices and start again!
